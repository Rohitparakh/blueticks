SME IPO

Service Overview

MSMEs (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) are the economic pillars of the country that has a significant impact on our growth, foreign trade and GDP. The Government recently initiated capital markets for SMEs with SME IPO to scale up and be financially responsible with due diligent financial management. 

For most SMEs, financing is the biggest challenge and the lack of it is the main reason for an SME going out of business. We strive hard to provide premium valuation and profile building so our SME partners can benefit from incentives and financial opportunities provided by SME Listing. 

BENEFITS OF SME LISTING

  • Funding Convenience

  • M&A currency

  • Premium Valuation

  • Visibility – Profile Building

  • Unlocking / Benchmarking Value

  • Incentive Mechanism for Employees

  • Benefits for Companies in Supply Chain

  • Governance – Internal Systems

  • Access to capital and financing opportunities

  • Entry & Exit Platforms for PE / Other Investors

FAQ

Some Questions & A.

What are the requirements to list on SME exchange?

  • The post-issue paid-up capital of the company should not exceed Rs. 25 Crores.

  • Positive Net Worth

  • Positive Cash accruals and Net Profit in the last 3 years

Is migration to Main Board Possible?

The companies listed on the SME exchange are allowed to migrate to the Main Board after meeting the listing requirements of the Main Board.

What is the listing fee on the SME platform?

The SME platform will have a one-time listing fee of Rs. 50,000/-.
